Sudoku is a logic-based puzzle where the goal is to fill a 9×9 grid so that every row, column, and 3×3 section contains the numbers 1 to 9 exactly once.

In this visualizer:
- Choose a difficulty level
- Fill the grid manually or use the automatic solver
- Check if your current solution is valid
- Watch the algorithm solve the puzzle step by step using backtracking

Sudoku is a great example of constraint solving and recursive problem-solving techniques.
