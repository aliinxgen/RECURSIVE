This project demonstrates a simple raycasting engine combined with pathfinding and exploration algorithms. Raycasting creates a 3D-style perspective by casting rays from the player's position and detecting walls in the maze.

In this visualizer:
- The player explores a maze from a first-person perspective
- The minimap shows walls, explored areas, and the treasure
- Press T to enable automatic exploration
- The AI uses pathfinding to navigate through the maze
- Press R to generate a new treasure location

This project combines concepts such as raycasting, A* pathfinding, maze exploration, and basic game engine rendering.
